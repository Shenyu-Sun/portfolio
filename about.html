about

This is Shenyu Sun, a UX designer. I believe every design should start from experience (human) and end with experience (product).

I made the transition to the digital product world at the beginning of 2021, when I just started my master's in urban studies and planning.
Within the 1 year transition. I have been mastering the fundamental UX design process, building digital products by coding, and Webflow development.
To me, I found designing for urban spaces and for digital products have 3 in common:
1) Human-centred: Creating a cosy space for citizens to have a better experience in the city is like designing the interfaces for a user-friendly experience while using products.
2) Storytelling: Persuasive storytelling is a communication tool for both Urban designers and UX designers to create a shared vocabulary
3) Design for the realisation: Working with the construction team and development team both contribute to the build/launch of a project.
